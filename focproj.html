About Tesla

Nikola Tesla (1856–1943) was a Serbian-American inventor and electrical engineer. He is famous for developing the modern alternating current (AC) electricity system.

He worked with Thomas Edison but later became his rival during the "War of Currents".

Major Inventions

  • Alternating Current (AC)
  • Tesla Coil
  • Wireless Power Transmission
  • Radio Technology
  • Induction Motor

Interesting Facts

  • Had a photographic memory
  • Visualized inventions in his mind
  • Spoke many languages
  • Obsessed with the number 3
  • Died poor despite major contributions
